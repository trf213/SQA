News





President of ServeYou Hospital, Jack Harris declares that ServeYou Hospital does not have a shortage in ventilators. When questioned by Barbados Today on Monday morning, he said that no patient entering the A&E will be turned back. He also added that a special entrance was allocated for any COVID-19 patients, so as not to interact with the other visitors that were there for other reasons.
