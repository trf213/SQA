Visting Hours



ServeYou is the only hospital in Barbados that offers 24 hours visitation. Ofcourse, depending o the condition of the patient, these hours may be amended.


Holiday Parties & Special Events
  • Party Rooms Available For Birthdays (maximum of five friends/family members
  • There is a general Christmas party annually for Non-Intensive Care patients. At this time families/friends (maximum of five per family) are allowed to attend.
