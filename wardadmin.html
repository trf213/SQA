Ward Information



ServeYou has been ranked # 1 by the Barbados Ministry of Health in patient care, comfort and cleanliness.

Below we listed information that will help you become acquainted to our wards, to prepare you for a warm, welcoming stay.

Qualified Friendly Staff

Our hospital comprises of some of the most highly trained doctors and nurses available throughout the Caribbean. We currently have the largest staff assigned to a hospital throughout the entire Caribbean and various locations Europe, Canada and the US. We also have the most diverse collection of nurses and doctors, coming from over 24 different countries worldwide.

Our staff knows that it is also as important to be friendly to our patients as it is to care for them competently. At ServeYou, you will never feel alone.

Nurses

Our nurses are available 24 hours daily to assist patients with any need.

  • At least two daily baths are given to our paitents
  • 24/7 surveillance is supplied to all wards with at least one nurse monitoring each room at all times, including holidays.
Doctors

At ServeYou, a highly qualified doctor is always available

  • Our doctors explain every condition and procedure with intimate detail, in a lanaguage that is easily understood
  • Our highly skilled surgeons practice the safest procedures, including avoiding unnecessary surgical precedures that put the paitent's life at risk

Clean Rooms & Beds

Bed sheets and pillow cases are changed daily, along with a daily mop and sweep of each ward room.

Visiting Hours

ServeYou is the only hospital in Barbados that offers 24 hours visitation. Ofcourse, depending o the condition of the patient, these hours may be amended.

Holiday Parties & Special Events
  • Party Rooms Available For Birthdays (maximum of five friends/family members
  • There is a general Christmas party annually for Non-Intensive Care patients. At this time families/friends (maximum of five per family) are allowed to attend.

Security

24/7 CCTV surveillance daily in every ward

Prompt Service

ServeYou has been ranked # 5 for shortest waiting period in an A&E globally.
